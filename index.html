Welcome to WSK

About Us

Winner Sumbiri Knitting (WSK) is a leading textile manufacturer specializing in high-quality warp and weft knitted fabrics. We supply to industries such as apparel, activewear, swimwear, and shapewear, with a focus on sustainable practices and cutting-edge technologies like KnitTech 3x1.

With a commitment to excellence, we are continually innovating and improving our production processes to meet the demands of a dynamic market while maintaining our dedication to eco-friendly practices.
